3. How we use your data

  • Deliver instant home-value estimates.
  • Send automated SMS updates (see “SMS Terms” below).
  • Connect you with licensed Realtors if you request it.
  • Improve our models, analytics, and fraud prevention.

4. Legal basis (GDPR / LGPD)

We process data on the basis of legitimate interests, contract performance, and your consent (for marketing texts).

5. Sharing your data

  • Realtor partners – only when you ask to be connected.
  • Service providers – cloud hosting, SMS gateway, analytics; bound by contract.
  • Regulators or law enforcement – where required by law.

We never sell your personal data to third parties for their own marketing.

6. SMS Terms

Program: automated texts deliver your estimate and, if requested, connect you with a Realtor.
Frequency: up to 10 messages per valuation request; may vary.
Fees: Msg & Data rates may apply.
Opt-out: reply STOP to cancel; HELP for help.
Privacy: mobile information is used solely to fulfill your request and is not shared for third-party marketing.

7. Cookies & tracking

We use first-party cookies and Google Analytics to understand site usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

8. Security

Data in transit is protected by TLS; at rest by AES-256. Access is role-based and logged.

9. Retention

Contact & property data: 5 years after last interaction.
SMS logs: 1 year.
We delete or anonymize thereafter unless a longer period is required by law.

10. Your rights

You may access, correct, delete, or export your data and withdraw consent at any time by emailing privacy@myrealvaluation.com. Residents of California, the EU, UK, and Brazil have additional statutory rights.

11. Children

Our service is not directed to children under 13; we do not knowingly collect data from them.

12. Changes to this policy

We will post changes here and update the “Last updated” date. Material changes will be announced via the app or SMS.
